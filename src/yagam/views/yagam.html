SREE MAHAGAYATHRI YAGAM 2016
1. Bharat Land witnessed Sree Maha Gayatri Yagam ,the greatest of all yagas ever held in the whole world in which great saints,vedic scholars and thantric Acharyas joined together who adore Bharat Culture and try their best to strengthen it with all their might !

2. VadakkeKunnathu Sree Rajarajeswari Devi Temple yard was the Yagam Land !The complete Gayatri Mantra is addressed to Sree Rajarajeswari Devi the subodiment of Brahma,Vishnu and Siva, the supreme power of the fourteen worlds who is really the VedaMatha Gayatri and Savitre Herself ! Thus come the Holi Astrological direction that Sree Maha Gayatri Yagam should be held at Sree Matha's Lotus Feet !

3. Sree Maha Gayatri Yagam was organised by Kollam Kadappakada Vadakke Kunnathu Sree Rajarajeswari Devi Temple Trust and Mookambika Mission Charitable Trust India hand in hand . The Yagam was conducted in the right Vedic Way from 18th April 2016 to 22nd April 2016 by a team of Vedic Scholars headed by this Holiness Sri Sri Sri Jayendra Puri Maha Swamiji , Sri Kailash Ashrama Maha Samsthana Rajarajeswari Nagar Bangalore along with his dear disciple Sri Advaithananda Puri Swami and guided by Sri Manjunatha Adiga the former Chief Priest of Kolloor Mookambika Temple and his son Sri Nithyananda Adiga , the Chief Chief Priest of Kolloor Mookambika Temple and his daughter-in-law Smt Rashitha Adiga as Lord and Lady of the Yagam.

4. The Well of the Yagam was lighted from the ever-burning lamp of Kolloor Mookambika Temple brought in a Chariot pageant,viewing about 350 great Temples en-rout along with Sree Matha's Idol and Maha Meru Nilayana after having performed great poojas in the Sanctum of Kolloor Mookambika Temple to be placed in the Yagam Land near the deity Veda Matha Sree Maha Gayatri Devi Herself!
